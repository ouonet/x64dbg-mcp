# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

This project aims to keep technical discussion direct, respectful, and useful.

Participants are expected to:

- Act professionally and respectfully
- Focus criticism on ideas, code, and behavior, not people
- Assume good intent while still being precise about problems
- Welcome contributors with different backgrounds and experience levels
- Avoid harassment, discrimination, intimidation, or personal attacks

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Abusive or demeaning language
- Harassment or unwanted contact
- Publishing private information without permission
- Bad-faith disruption of discussions, reviews, or issue threads
- Any conduct that makes participation unsafe or unreasonably hostile

## Scope

This applies to repository discussions, issues, pull requests, reviews, and other project spaces.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, moderate, lock, or remove content that violates this code of conduct, and may restrict participation when necessary to protect the project community.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainers through a private channel when possible.

For security-related concerns, use the process in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
